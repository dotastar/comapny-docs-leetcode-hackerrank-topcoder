Given a amount and several denominations of coins, find all 
possible ways that amount can be formed?
eg amount = 5, denominations = 1,2,3. 
Ans- 5 ways 
1) 1,1,1,1,1 
2) 1,1,1,2 (combinations aren't counted eg 1,2,1,1 etc) 
3) 1,1,3 
4) 1,2,2 
5) 2,3
